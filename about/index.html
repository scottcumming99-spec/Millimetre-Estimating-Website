About Millimetre Estimating Limited

A professional estimating and quantity surveying business built around clarity and trust.

Millimetre Estimating Limited is a Glasgow-based construction estimating and quantity surveying consultancy serving clients across Scotland and the UK through a practical, responsive online service model.

Who We Are

A commercial partner for projects, tenders, and procurement decisions.

The business is set up to support clients who need stronger commercial control, clearer pricing, and better structured information at the points where accuracy and presentation matter most.

We work with developers, contractors, specialist contractors, and homeowners who want a dependable estimating and quantity surveying service delivered with professionalism and measured commercial judgement.

Business Position

Based in Glasgow and operating nationally through an online delivery model, the business combines the responsiveness of a modern consultancy with the standards of a traditional professional service.

That means clear communication, defined scope, practical outputs, and a service that is focused on helping clients make better commercial decisions.

What We Value

The principles behind the way the business works.

Accuracy

Commercial advice and estimating outputs that are precise, clear, and decision-ready.

Professionalism

A calm and measured service that reflects the environment clients operate in.

Responsiveness

An efficient online service model built to support live project and tender deadlines.

Who We Work With

Support tailored to the client, sector, and procurement route.

  • Developers seeking cost planning and procurement support.
  • Contractors pricing live tenders and return opportunities.
  • Specialist contractors requiring accurate package pricing.
  • Homeowners needing estimating guidance on residential projects.
Why Glasgow Matters

Locally based, nationally capable.

Being based in Glasgow gives the business a strong regional position in Scotland, while the online model means enquiries, documents, drawings, and tender information can be reviewed and progressed efficiently wherever the project is located.
