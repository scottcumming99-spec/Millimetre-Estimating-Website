About Us

A Scotland-based estimating business for contractors across the UK.

Millimetre Estimating Limited supports contractors who need clear estimating input, stronger tender pricing, and practical commercial support across a range of construction sectors.

How We Work

Support shaped by real pricing and tender requirements.

The business is built around practical pricing, tendering, and commercial requirements rather than generic consultancy language. The emphasis is on clarity, responsiveness, and useful outputs.

Estimating is the lead service, supported by tendering, bid input, and wider commercial support where contractors need better visibility before submitting or progressing work.

Based in Scotland

Scotland is the main geographic focus, but the service is set up to support projects across the wider UK through efficient online communication and document-led review.

That means clear communication, a defined scope, and practical outputs that help contractors make better commercial decisions.

Sector Familiarity

Experience across the sectors contractors ask us about most often.

Support is offered across civils, domestic construction, commercial construction, structures, airside, railside, and maritime work, allowing the business to respond to varied contractor requirements.

Structures, Airside, Railside, Maritime
