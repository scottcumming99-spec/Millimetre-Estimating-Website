Cookie Policy

Cookie policy for Millimetre Estimating Limited.

This page explains how cookies may be used on the website and how those settings can be managed through your browser and consent choices.

Scope of This Policy

  1. Millimetre Estimating Limited uses cookies when you visit our website, www.millimetre.ltd, to help customise the Website and improve your experience using it.
  2. This policy applies between you, the user of this Website, and Millimetre Estimating Limited, the owner and provider of this Website.
  3. When you visit the Website, and before the Website places cookies on your computer, you will be presented with a message bar requesting your consent to set those cookies. By giving consent, you enable us to provide a better experience and service. You may deny consent, although certain features of the Website may not function fully or as intended.
  4. This cookie policy should be read alongside, and in addition to, our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

  1. A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer by this Website when you visit certain parts of the Website and/or use certain features of the Website.
  2. This Website may place and access certain cookies on your computer. We use these cookies to improve your experience and to improve our range of services.
  3. Cookies do not usually contain information that personally identifies you. However, personal information we store about you may be linked to information obtained from and stored in cookies. For more information on how personal information is handled and stored, refer to our Privacy Policy.

Types of Cookies

  1. This Website uses the following cookies:
    1. Strictly necessary cookies required for the operation of the Website.
    2. Analytical or performance cookies that help us recognise and count visitors and understand how they move around the Website.
    3. Functionality cookies used to recognise you when you return to the Website and remember your preferences.
    4. Targeting cookies that record your visit, the pages viewed, and the links followed in order to make content and advertising more relevant.
  2. You can find a list of the cookies we use in the attached Cookie Schedule.
  3. We have carefully chosen these cookies and have taken steps to ensure that your privacy is protected and respected at all times.

How to Control Your Cookies

  1. You can choose to enable or disable cookies in your internet browser. By default, most internet browsers accept cookies, but this can be changed.
  2. You can switch off cookies at any time, although you may lose information that enables faster and more efficient Website access.
  3. It is recommended that you keep your browser up to date and consult the guidance provided by your browser developer if you are unsure about adjusting your privacy settings.
  4. For more information generally on cookies, including how to disable them, please refer to aboutcookies.org. You will also find details there on how to delete cookies from your computer.

Changes to This Policy

  1. Millimetre Estimating Limited reserves the right to change this cookie policy as may be necessary from time to time or as required by law. Any changes will be immediately posted on the Website and you are deemed to have accepted the terms of the cookie policy on your first use of the Website following the changes.
