Services

Estimating, tendering, bid support, and quantity surveying services.

Millimetre Estimating Limited provides structured commercial support to help clients price work more effectively, prepare stronger submissions, and gain clearer visibility over project costs and procurement decisions.

Core Services

A commercial offer built around the work clients most often need.

Construction Estimating

Detailed take-offs, pricing preparation, budget reviews, and cost information for projects and tenders.

Tendering Support

Support for live opportunities, tender preparation, and commercially stronger return submissions.

Technical and Cost Returns

Clear coordination of pricing, technical input, and structured return information.

Bid Writing Support

Written bid input that helps improve submission quality and overall presentation.

Quantity Surveying

Cost planning, Bills of Quantities, commercial review, and wider project support.

Online Estimating Service

A responsive online service for clients who need fast, document-led commercial input.

Client Types

Support aligned to the way different clients procure and deliver work.

Different clients require different levels of detail, reporting, and commercial input. The service is therefore tailored to the project stage, procurement route, and the practical decisions that need to be made.

  • Contractors pricing tenders and managing submission quality.
  • Specialist contractors preparing accurate package pricing.
