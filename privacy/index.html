Data Collected

  5. Even if we delete your Data, it may persist on backup or archival media for legal, tax or regulatory purposes.

  1. You have rights in relation to your Data, including the right to access, correct, erase, restrict our use of your Data, request data portability, and object to our use of your Data.
  2. To make enquiries, exercise your rights, or withdraw consent where consent is our legal basis, please contact info@millimetre.ltd.
